How it all started

Having worked in a stone quarry after losing my job, I interacted with a couple of women with whom I shared similar challenges. Some were widows, divorced and others lived under extreme conditions of poverty. They could not provide all the basic needs for their families'education inclusive. We came together and up with the idea of opening up a Nursery school "Bright Future Infant School" on 3rd February 2003 that later evolved into a Bright Future Infant and Primary School.

Mission

Our mission is to provide high standards of learning experiences to learners in a secure and caring environment. We are committed to producing literate and competent students who qualify for secondary education.
